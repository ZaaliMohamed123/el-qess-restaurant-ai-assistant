# El-Qess Moroccan Restaurant
## About Us
El-Qess, meaning "The Story" in Arabic, is an authentic Moroccan restaurant dedicated to bringing the rich flavors, warm hospitality, and vibrant culture of Morocco to your table. Every dish we serve tells a story of tradition passed down through generations, prepared with love and the finest ingredients.
Our chefs, trained in traditional Moroccan culinary arts, create dishes that transport you to the bustling souks of Marrakech, the coastal kitchens of Casablanca, and the mountain villages of the Atlas.

## Dietary Information
### Halal Certification
El-Qess is 100% halal certified. All meats are sourced from certified halal suppliers.
### Vegetarian Options
We offer several vegetarian dishes including:
- Couscous aux Légumes
- Zaalouk
- Taktouka
- Hummus El-Qess
- Vegetable Tagine (available on request)
### Vegan Options
Many dishes can be prepared vegan upon request. Please inform your server.
### Gluten-Free Options
Several dishes are naturally gluten-free or can be modified. Please ask your server for recommendations.
### Allergen Information
Please inform your server of any allergies. Our kitchen uses nuts, dairy, gluten, and sesame.
